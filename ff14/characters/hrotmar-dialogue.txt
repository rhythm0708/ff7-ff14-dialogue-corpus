Why'd I join the Resistance? Well, if you must know...it was to avenge a friend the imperials butchered before my very eyes, back in Ala Mhigo.
We owned a stall in the markets, and a passing soldier snatched up a piece of fruit. He spoke out before I could stop him, only to get a fulm of steel in the gut for his troubles. I found my way here, and that was that.
